About

I am a PhD candidate in International Relations at the Indian Institute of Technology Madras, where my research examines China's periphery diplomacy, the Belt & Road Initiative, and the shifting dynamics of great-power competition in the Indo-Pacific.

My work spans geopolitics, digital infrastructure diplomacy, transboundary water governance, and South Asian regional politics. I combine policy-oriented analysis with rigorous academic inquiry, publishing in journals such as The Round Table, Asian Studies Review, and The Diplomat.
