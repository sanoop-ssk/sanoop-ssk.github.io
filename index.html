About

I am a PhD candidate in International Relations at the Indian Institute of Technology Madras, focusing on geopolitical trends, cross-cultural engagement, and youth's role in global affairs. My research examines China's periphery diplomacy, the Belt & Road Initiative, and shifting dynamics of great-power competition in the Indo-Pacific.

Passionate about youth empowerment and intercultural understanding through academic research and grassroots initiatives. I believe in young leaders' potential for sustainable diplomacy — contributing research experience and a collaborative spirit to global policy dialogues.

My work spans digital infrastructure diplomacy, transboundary water governance, and South Asian regional politics, publishing in journals such as The Round Table, Asian Studies Review, and The Diplomat.
