About Me

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in International Relations at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Madras. My research investigates the geopolitical and economic dimensions of China’s Belt and Road Initiative (BRI) in South Asia, analyzing infrastructure finance, debt sustainability, and periphery diplomacy.

As I look toward my postdoctoral career, my focus is expanding to encompass digital infrastructure diplomacy, the Digital Silk Road, and AI governance within the Indo-Pacific region. I hold an MPA in Public Administration from IGNOU and an MA in Political Science from the University of Kerala.

I have been recognized as a Research Fellow at the Emerging Leaders Forum (Indo-Pacific Study Centre, Australia) and have been awarded the Chinese Government Scholarship at Beijing Normal University.
